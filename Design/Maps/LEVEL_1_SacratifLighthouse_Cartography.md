# Sacratif Lighthouse Cartography Document

### Overview
This document provides a detailed map and layout for Sacratif Lighthouse, located in Level 1 of the SilentCoast1 game. The area features various challenges, items to collect, and enemies to face.  

---

## Map Layout
![Sacratif Lighthouse Map](path/to/sacratif_lighthouse_map_image.png)

### Areas
1. **Exterior Grounds**  
   - Description: The area surrounding the lighthouse includes rocky outcrops and waves crashing against the shore.  
   - Enemies: Seagull Swarm
   - Items: Health Pack, Flashlight

2. **Lighthouse Entrance**  
   - Description: A large wooden door leads into the lighthouse.  
   - Enemies: None
   - Items: Key to the Lighthouse

3. **First Floor**  
   - Description: Contains a reception area, maps of the surrounding waters, and a staircase leading up.  
   - Enemies: Shadow Wraith
   - Puzzles: Find the hidden lever behind the map board to unlock the second floor.
   - Items: Vintage Compass

4. **Second Floor**  
   - Description: The floor has a central chamber with windows providing a view of the coast.  
   - Enemies: The Warden
   - Puzzles: Solve the riddle inscribed on the wall to access the lighthouse's light chamber.
   - Items: Lantern

5. **Light Chamber**  
   - Description: The topmost room of the lighthouse where the light mechanism is located.  
   - Enemies: None
   - Puzzles: Activate the light mechanism to reveal a hidden passage.
   - Items: Beacon Frequency Tuner

---

### Puzzle Details
- **Lever Puzzle on First Floor**:  
  - **Objective**: Uncover the hidden lever to gain access to the second floor. 
  - **Hints**: Check behind the nautical maps.

- **Riddle on Second Floor**:  
  - **Riddle**: "I speak without a mouth and hear without ears. I have no body, but I come alive with the wind. What am I?"  
  - **Answer**: An Echo  

---

### Item Locations
- **Health Pack**: Found on a rock near the water's edge.
- **Key to the Lighthouse**: Inside the mailbox by the entrance.
- **Vintage Compass**: On the desk in the reception area on the first floor.
- **Lantern**: Behind the bookshelf on the second floor.
- **Beacon Frequency Tuner**: Inside the light mechanism on the top floor.

---

### Enemy Placement
- **Seagull Swarm**: Spawns randomly outside on the exterior grounds.
- **Shadow Wraith**: Appears after the player collects the Vintage Compass on the first floor.
- **The Warden**: A challenging enemy that guards the second floor, becomes active after the riddle is solved.

### Atmospheric Details
- **Lighting**: Dimly lit with flickering lanterns, creating a sense of unease.
- **Sound**: Ambient sounds of waves crashing and ghostly whispers throughout.
- **Weather Effects**: Misty with occasional rain, contributing to a gloomy atmosphere.
